GOSPL Project

This page provides the scientific background and goals of the GOSPL project.

Background

Medical literature on overgrowth syndromes (OGs) describes them as a highly heterogeneous group of conditions, which show a common feature of excessive growth. Timely diagnosis and clinical management implementation are crucial for patients with OGs due to an increased neoplasia risk.

Project goals

The project aims to investigate the genetic drivers of overgrowth syndromes and generate diagnostic algorithm and personalised follow-up plans for neoplasia prevention.

We will be using state of the art molecular genetic techniques, including whole exome sequencing to identify common and novel variants in patients with OGs in Lithuanian and Polish populations.

As the project develops, key findings will be shared witht the scientific community and the public.
