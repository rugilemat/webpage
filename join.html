Join the Project

Patients

The project offers an extensive medical examination plan for the patients with diagnosed/suspected overgrowth syndromes and/or features. We expect most of the patients to be referred to us from other medical professionals and would like to invite them to reach out to us with any patients they think would qualify.

If you or your family member is affected by overgrowth syndrome and would like to participate in this project, please reach out to us via email gospl@mf.vu.lt.

Researchers

The project is currently not recruiting any more scientific staff. However, we highly welcome collaborations with other groups, working on overgrowth syndromes.
