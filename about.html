About the project

GOSPL is a collaborative project between Vilnius University (Lithuania) and Bialystok Univeristy (Poland).

What is GOSPL?

Genetic Background of Overgrowth Syndromes in Polish and Lithuanian Populations: basis for rapid genetic testing to prevent neoplasias (GOSPL) project is a collaboration between Vilnius University in Lithuania and Bialystok University in Poland.

The three-year project is funded by the Lithuania-Poland bilateral research funding programme. It was started in June 2021. The project aims to determine the genetic background of OGs in Polish and Lithuanian populations to develop diagnostic algorithms and optimize healthcare strategy for neoplasia prevention.

Vilnius University

The research team at the Deparment of Human and Medical Genetics (Institute of Biomedical Sciences, Faculty of Medicine) are the Lithuanian project partner. They combine the clinical expertise of medical doctors, specialising in rare disorders with experience of molecular geneticists and bioinformaticians.

Bialystok University

The research team at Bialystok University is the Polish partner of the project. They include physicians, molecular biologists and bioinformaticians.
